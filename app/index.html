About this Publication

Occupy Hong Kong and Occupy Central are civil disobedience campaigns in Hong Kong, China. The Occupy Central with Love and Peace organization intends to pressure the PRC government in China into granting an electoral system that "satisf[ies] the international standards in relation to universal suffrage" in the Hong Kong Chief Executive election in 2017.

OCLP is fighting for equal sufferage in Hong Kong through Civil Disobedience means, mainly through the non-violent occupation of Central. Please join us and help spread the word, love and peace.
